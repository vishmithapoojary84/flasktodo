About This To-Do Website

Welcome to my personal To-Do website! This project was created as a way to help you manage tasks, stay organized, and keep track of your daily goals. As a solo developer, I wanted to build a simple, easy-to-use platform that can help people like you get things done efficiently.

My Story

I built this website out of a personal need to stay organized. As someone juggling multiple tasks, I realized there was a need for a simple tool that could help me stay on top of my daily to-do list. Rather than using complex task management systems, I wanted something straightforward and user-friendly. And that's how this website came to life!

Features

  • Create, edit, and delete tasks
  • Set due dates and deadlines
  • Simple, minimalistic design
  • Track your progress and stay productive
